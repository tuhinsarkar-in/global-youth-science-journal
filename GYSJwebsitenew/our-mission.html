Our Mission

The mission of the Global Youth Science Journal (GYS Journal) is to empower and inspire young researchers worldwide by providing a free, open-access, and peer-reviewed platform for sharing innovative ideas and scientific discoveries. We are dedicated to making research accessible to all, regardless of background, and to nurturing the next generation of scientists, thinkers, and creators.

  • To offer a global, non-profit, and inclusive journal for young researchers aged 12 to 20.
  • To ensure fast, free, and open publication of research in science, computer science, social science, arts, and literature.
  • To maintain high standards through rigorous peer review and editorial oversight.
  • To support the publication of research articles spanning diverse fields, including science, art, technology, and management.
  • To promote open access, ensuring that research is available freely and widely online.
  • To encourage creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration among young minds globally.

We believe that by supporting and showcasing the work of young researchers, we are shaping a better tomorrow and empowering the next generation of scientists and innovators.
