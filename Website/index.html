IYS Journal - Call for Research Papers 2024

The International Youth Science Journal (IYS Journal) is a peer-reviewed, open-access research platform that maintains high standards through rigorous peer review and editorial oversight. Our journal promotes open access, ensuring that research is available freely and widely online. IYS Journal supports the publication of research articles spanning diverse fields, including science, art, technology, and management.

All original research articles published by the IYS Journal are made available online with full text immediately upon release, ensuring free access. Authors retain the copyright to their work while granting IYS Journal the rights necessary for publication and unrestricted citation. Additionally, in line with the mission of the International Youth Science Journal, we offer free publication services for researchers under the age of 20. All published papers are indexed and archived across various open and subscription-based platforms.

- Tuhin Sarkar
